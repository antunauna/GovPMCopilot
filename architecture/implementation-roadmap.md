# Implementation Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation

- Build policy and enterprise data models.
- Establish policy knowledge base and enterprise material knowledge base.
- Define rule-based eligibility checks.

## Phase 2: Core skills

- Deliver PolicyRadar and EntityMatcher.
- Deliver PolicyInterpreter and opportunity card generation.
- Deliver ProposalComposer templates.

## Phase 3: Governance and quality

- Deliver ReviewAuditor scorecards.
- Deliver WorkflowOrchestrator states, reminders, and versioning.
- Add audit logs, human review gates, and lifecycle monitoring.

## Phase 4: Scale-out

- Add more project lines and regional policy trees.
- Add analytics, reuse metrics, and historical pattern mining.
