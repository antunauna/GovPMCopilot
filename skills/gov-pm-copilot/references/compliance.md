# Compliance Principles

## Hard rules

- Do not fabricate policy terms, funding amounts, dates, certifications, KPI evidence, or enterprise facts.
- Do not let the model override hard qualification gates.
- Do not present uncertain facts as confirmed conclusions.

## Required controls

- Preserve source links and evidence snippets for key facts.
- Distinguish facts, inferences, and recommendations.
- Require human confirmation for sensitive financial and legal content.
- Keep audit logs for high-risk actions.

## High-risk scenarios

- Repeated applications for the same build content.
- Conflicting entity ownership or qualification evidence.
- Outdated or superseded policy guidance.
- KPI and budget inflation not grounded in enterprise evidence.
