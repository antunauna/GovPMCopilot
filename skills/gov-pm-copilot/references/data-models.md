# Shared Data Models

GovPMCopilot uses four primary shared objects.

## Policy

Represents a policy notice, guide, rule, or supporting measure with source metadata, lifecycle status, key conditions, and traceable evidence.

## Entity

Represents a legal entity or subsidiary with registration facts, operating profile, financial and R&D indicators, certifications, IP assets, and regional applicability.

## MatchResult

Represents the result of entity-to-policy evaluation, including hard-pass checks, score, gap analysis, and recommendation rationale.

## Opportunity

Represents an actionable application opportunity combining a policy, recommended entity, strategic direction, owner, workflow state, and next actions.
