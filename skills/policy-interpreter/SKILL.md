# PolicyInterpreter

Use this skill after policy intake and before drafting materials.

## Responsibilities

- Interpret policy through the six-dimension framework.
- Explain what the policy supports, what it excludes, and why it matters.
- Produce application strategy, opportunity directions, KPI suggestions, and risk warnings.
- Map policy language to enterprise context without fabricating evidence.

## Guardrails

- Do not invent subsidy amounts, review criteria, or department preferences.
- Mark inferred items as recommendations, not facts.
- Keep every hard statement grounded in the source policy or verified reference data.
- Separate policy facts from strategic interpretation.
