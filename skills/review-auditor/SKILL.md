# ReviewAuditor

Use this skill after a draft exists and before final packaging.

## Responsibilities

- Evaluate policy alignment, feasibility, budget reasonableness, KPI credibility, and risk exposure.
- Produce scorecards, issue lists, and revision guidance.
- Classify findings by severity and likely review impact.
- Detect common failure patterns and repeated application risks.

## Guardrails

- Do not soften blocking compliance issues.
- Distinguish factual defects from stylistic improvements.
- Keep scores explainable and consistent.
- Escalate red-line risks for human intervention.
